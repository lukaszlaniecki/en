My Pivots

01.

"A generic structure can make even a powerful narrative feel ordinary."

For over two decades, I have built a career around a singular core ability: the creative transformation of ideas into tangible, successful outcomes. My professional life has been a dynamic blend of logical rigor and creative intuition, beginning with a long tenure as a legal professional.

In that role, I honed my skills in crafting compelling arguments, identifying unseen strengths and weaknesses, and settling disputes out of court. I often saw third and fourth options when others were locked into two. I even applied this thinking in a personal context, when I successfully resolved a highly contentious family conflict by suggesting we change our environment and hold our discussions while walking our dog. The change in perspective proved to be the key to communication.

02.

My creative side, however, has always been an equally powerful driver. It was this passion that led me to launch a blog on conflict resolution—one of the first of its kind in Poland—which quickly became a foundation for new projects. My ability to look at things from a broad perspective, connecting disparate ideas to spot opportunities, has manifested in projects ranging from the co-creation of the Legal Market Day event to the launch of the LinkedIn Poland Women community, a highly successful and influential publication that came from a completely different initial idea.

"I’m a builder and an operator with a track record of transforming a vision into a valuable reality."
